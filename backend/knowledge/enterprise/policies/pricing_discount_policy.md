# NimbusCRM — Pricing & Discount Policy

## Overview
This document governs all discount and pricing decisions for NimbusCRM contracts. All discounts must be approved through the correct channel before being communicated to the customer.

## Standard Pricing Tiers

| Tier | Per Seat/Month | Minimum Seats | Key Features |
|---|---|---|---|
| Starter | $49 | 5 | Pipeline, Contacts, Email |
| Professional | $99 | 10 | + Reporting, Automation, Integrations |
| Enterprise | $149 | 25 | + AI Insights, API Access, Dedicated CSM |
| Enterprise Plus | $199 | 50 | + Custom SLAs, Priority Support, SSO |

*Annual billing required for all tiers above Starter.*

## Approved Discount Levels by Role

| Approver | Max Discount | Conditions |
|---|---|---|
| CSM | 5% | Renewal only, must be approved in writing |
| CSM Manager | 15% | Renewal or expansion, documented business reason required |
| VP Customer Success | 25% | Strategic accounts, must include exec alignment |
| CEO | > 25% | Exceptional cases only, board-level accounts |

## Approved Discount Scenarios

### Renewal Discount
- Available for customers renewing 60+ days before expiry
- Maximum: 10% without manager approval
- Must be tied to multi-year commitment (2+ years) for discounts >10%

### Early Renewal Incentive
- Customers who renew 90+ days early qualify for price lock (no annual price increase)
- Can be combined with up to 5% discount

### Expansion Discount
- Customers adding 10+ seats in a single order qualify for 8% seat discount on new seats only
- Customers adding 25+ seats qualify for 12% on new seats

### Distressed Account Discount
- Accounts with health score < 40 and renewal within 60 days
- CSM Manager may approve up to 20% temporary discount for a 1-year extension
- Must be paired with a documented recovery plan (joint success plan required)

### Hardship / Force Majeure
- Case-by-case basis, requires VP approval
- Documentation of customer's financial situation required

## What Is NOT Permitted
- Discounts communicated verbally without written approval
- Discounts on month-to-month contracts
- Retroactive discounts on closed contracts
- Combining multiple discount categories without VP approval

## Contract Amendment Process
All discounts must be reflected in a formal contract amendment signed by both parties before the discount takes effect. CSMs must not promise discounts before approval is obtained.
