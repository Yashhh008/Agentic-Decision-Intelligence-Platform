# NimbusCRM — Frequently Asked Questions

## General

**Q: How many users can I have on NimbusCRM?**
A: Your licensed user count is defined in your contract. You can add users at any time by contacting your CSM or through the billing portal. Additional users are billed on a pro-rata monthly basis.

**Q: What happens if we exceed our licensed user count?**
A: You will receive an automated notification at 90% capacity. Your CSM will reach out to discuss expansion options. The platform will not cut off access but overages will appear on the next invoice.

## Onboarding & Adoption

**Q: Our team is not adopting the platform. What should we do?**
A: Low adoption is one of the most common challenges. We recommend:
1. Identify your internal champion and provide them with admin training
2. Start with one core use case (e.g., pipeline management) before expanding
3. Use the NimbusCRM onboarding wizard (Settings > Onboarding)
4. Schedule a free adoption enablement session with your CSM

**Q: How long does onboarding typically take?**
A: Most teams reach baseline adoption (60%+ active users) within 45-60 days. Complex migrations or integrations may take 90 days. Enterprise onboarding specialists are available for accounts above $50k ARR.

**Q: The reporting module is confusing for non-technical users. What help is available?**
A: We offer role-based training specifically for non-technical users. Contact your CSM to schedule a reporting workshop. We also recommend the "Reports for Business Users" guide in our product documentation.

## Integrations

**Q: Which integrations are available?**
A: NimbusCRM integrates with:
- Salesforce, HubSpot (CRM sync)
- Jira, Asana (project management)
- Slack, Microsoft Teams (notifications)
- Zendesk, Intercom (support tickets)
- Google Workspace, Microsoft 365 (email and calendar)

**Q: We're having trouble with the ERP integration. Who do we contact?**
A: Contact technical support at support@nimbuscrm.com or open a P1 ticket for active integration failures. Enterprise customers have access to a dedicated integration engineer.

## Renewals & Billing

**Q: When should we expect renewal discussions?**
A: Your CSM will initiate renewal conversations 90 days before your contract end date. If you have not heard from us by 60 days before renewal, please reach out proactively.

**Q: Can we negotiate pricing at renewal?**
A: Loyalty discounts are available for customers who renew on time and maintain a health score above 60. Speak with your CSM for specific options.
