# NimbusCRM Internal Policy — Escalation & Risk Classification

**Document Type:** Internal Policy  
**Owner:** Customer Success Leadership  
**Effective:** January 2026  

---

## 1. Risk Classification Framework

All NimbusCRM customer accounts are classified into three risk tiers:

| Tier | Health Score | Action SLA |
|---|---|---|
| 🟢 Healthy | 70–100 | Standard QBR cadence |
| 🟡 At Risk | 40–69 | Proactive outreach within 5 business days |
| 🔴 Critical | 0–39 | CSM escalation within 24 hours; VP notification |

---

## 2. Escalation Triggers

The following conditions **automatically escalate** an account to Critical status:

1. **Health score drops below 40** — immediate CSM notification
2. **Renewal date within 30 days + health score below 60** — VP of CS notification required
3. **Open support ticket >14 days unresolved at High or Critical severity** — Engineering Director and CSM VP notified
4. **Active users fall below 40% of licensed seats** — Adoption Intervention required
5. **Champion departure confirmed** — Executive Outreach Protocol activated within 48 hours
6. **Customer explicitly mentions competitor evaluation** — Account must be flagged as "Churn Risk" and escalated same day

---

## 3. Churn Prevention Protocol

Upon flagging an account as Churn Risk:

**Within 24 hours:**
- CSM schedules emergency account review call
- CSM prepares Business Value Report (usage stats, ROI data)
- CSM notifies VP of Customer Success

**Within 48 hours:**
- VP of Customer Success reviews account
- Decision: assign Churn Prevention Specialist or handle directly
- Executive outreach letter sent if deal value >$50k

**Within 72 hours:**
- Customized retention offer prepared (may include discount, feature unlock, or dedicated support upgrade)
- Offer requires VP approval for discounts >15%

---

## 4. Executive Engagement Protocol

**When to invoke:**
- Renewal >$50k AND health score <50
- Champion resigned from key account
- Customer explicitly threatens non-renewal in writing
- Account has been "At Risk" for more than 60 consecutive days

**Who contacts the customer:**
- VP of Customer Success contacts customer's Director/VP equivalent
- Chief Customer Officer contacts C-suite for accounts >$100k

---

## 5. Renewal Decision Timeline

Customers must receive a formal renewal proposal **no later than 45 days before renewal date**.

If the customer has not acknowledged the renewal by 30 days before the renewal date, the account is automatically escalated to "Renewal Risk" status and assigned to the Renewal Specialist team.

---

## 6. SLA Summary for CSMs

| Situation | Response Requirement |
|---|---|
| Health score drops to Critical | 24-hour outreach |
| Ticket >14 days unresolved | Same-day internal escalation |
| Champion resigns | 48-hour executive outreach |
| Customer mentions leaving | Same-day risk flag + VP notification |
| Renewal <30 days, no renewal signed | Immediate Renewal Specialist assignment |
