# NimbusCRM Customer Onboarding Playbook

## Purpose
Guide new customers to product value within the first 90 days.

## Onboarding Phases

### Phase 1: Week 1-2 — Foundation
- Conduct kickoff call with all key stakeholders
- Complete technical setup and SSO configuration
- Import existing CRM data (migration support available)
- Assign dedicated onboarding specialist

### Phase 2: Week 3-6 — Adoption
- Conduct role-based training sessions:
  - Administrators: 4-hour deep dive
  - End users: 2-hour workshop
  - Managers: 1-hour reporting overview
- Set up automated workflows for top 3 use cases
- Enable email and calendar integrations

### Phase 3: Week 7-12 — Expansion
- Review adoption metrics with customer
- Identify power users for internal champions program
- Enable advanced features (AI Insights, Custom Reports)
- Schedule first Quarterly Business Review (QBR)

## Low Adoption Warning Signs
- Fewer than 50% of licensed users active after 30 days
- No data imported after 14 days
- No custom workflows created after 45 days

## Recommended Actions for Low Adoption
1. Schedule urgent adoption enablement session
2. Assign additional onboarding specialist
3. Offer free training credits
4. Simplify initial use case to one core workflow

## Success Criteria
- 80%+ of licensed users active by day 60
- At least 3 automated workflows created
- Customer satisfaction score above 8/10
