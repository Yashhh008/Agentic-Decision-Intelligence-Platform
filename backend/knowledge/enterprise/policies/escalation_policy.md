# NimbusCRM — Escalation Policy

## Purpose
This policy defines when and how to escalate customer issues within NimbusCRM's Customer Success organization.

## Escalation Tiers

### Tier 1: CSM-Level (Health Score 60-100)
- Handled by assigned Customer Success Manager
- Response time: 24 hours
- Standard check-in cadence: Monthly

### Tier 2: Manager-Level (Health Score 40-60)
- CSM involves CS Manager
- Response time: 4 hours
- Increased check-in cadence: Bi-weekly
- Recovery plan required within 5 business days

### Tier 3: Director-Level Escalation (Health Score 30-40)
- CS Director or VP involved
- Response time: 2 hours
- Executive-to-executive call required within 5 business days
- 30-day recovery plan mandatory

### Tier 4: Executive Escalation (Health Score below 30)
- CEO or CRO involvement for accounts above $75k ARR
- Response time: 1 hour
- Same-day executive outreach required
- Custom recovery terms may be offered

## Escalation Triggers (Regardless of Health Score)
- Customer explicitly mentions cancellation
- Champion resigns and no replacement identified
- More than 5 unresolved support tickets
- Missed 2+ consecutive scheduled calls
- Renewal within 14 days with no signed commitment

## Documentation Requirements
All escalations must be documented in the CRM with:
- Root cause summary
- Actions taken
- Expected resolution timeline
- Owner and escalation approver
