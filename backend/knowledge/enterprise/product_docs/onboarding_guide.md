# NimbusCRM — Product Documentation

## Feature Guide: Onboarding Wizard

**Version:** 2.4  
**Audience:** New users, Customer Success Managers, IT Administrators  

---

### Overview

The NimbusCRM Onboarding Wizard guides new users through a 5-step setup process to configure their workspace, integrate existing data, and complete role-based training before first active use.

---

### Step 1 — Account Setup

Users set their display name, time zone, and notification preferences. Administrators can pre-configure these settings via the Admin Panel before inviting users.

**Common Issues:**
- Users skipping this step results in missing notification alerts later
- Time zone mismatch causes reporting discrepancies for distributed teams

---

### Step 2 — Role Assignment

Users are assigned a role by their organization administrator. Roles control access permissions, visible modules, and available reporting features.

**Available Roles:**
- Account Executive
- Customer Success Manager
- Sales Manager
- Revenue Operations
- Administrator

**Note:** If a user gets stuck at this step, the issue is usually that no Administrator has been assigned in the account. An Admin seat must be activated before user roles can be distributed.

---

### Step 3 — Data Import

Users can optionally import existing contacts, accounts, and deal data from:
- CSV upload (bulk)
- Salesforce export
- HubSpot export
- Manual entry

**Best Practice:** Import a test batch of 10–20 records first to verify field mapping before full import.

---

### Step 4 — Workflow Configuration

Users configure their first sales or customer success workflow. Templates available:
- New Customer Onboarding
- Deal Pipeline
- Renewal Tracking
- Support Escalation

---

### Step 5 — Training Completion

Users complete a 20-minute guided tour of core features. Completion is tracked and visible in the Admin adoption dashboard.

**CSM Insight:** Customers who complete Step 5 within the first 7 days show 3x higher 90-day retention vs. those who skip training.

---

### Adoption Best Practices for CSMs

1. **Schedule a dedicated onboarding kickoff call** within the first week
2. **Identify a power user** in the customer team who can champion internal adoption
3. **Set a 30-day adoption milestone** — target 60% of licensed seats active within 30 days
4. **Use the Adoption Dashboard** (Admin → Analytics → User Activity) to monitor progress
5. **Follow up at day 14** if adoption is below 40%
6. **Escalate to Customer Success Manager** if adoption is below 30% at day 30

---

### Low Adoption Recovery Playbook (Summary)

If a customer's adoption rate falls below 50%:

1. Book a Business Value Review
2. Run a targeted workshop for the specific team struggling with adoption
3. Identify and remove workflow blockers
4. Assign a dedicated onboarding specialist if needed
5. Consider a platform health check to identify configuration issues
