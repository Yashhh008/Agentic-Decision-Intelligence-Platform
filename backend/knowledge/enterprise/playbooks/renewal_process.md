# NimbusCRM Renewal Process Playbook

## Purpose
Ensure consistent and successful contract renewals.

## Renewal Timeline

### 90 Days Before Renewal
- Begin formal renewal preparation
- Review health score and adoption metrics
- Identify renewal risks and strengths
- Schedule executive business review

### 60 Days Before Renewal
- Present annual value summary to customer
- Discuss contract options and potential upgrades
- Identify expansion opportunities
- Confirm budget approval process with champion

### 30 Days Before Renewal
- Send formal renewal proposal
- If health score is below 50: activate churn prevention protocol
- If champion is new: schedule executive introduction meeting
- Escalate to VP if renewal is at risk

### 14 Days Before Renewal
- Final confirmation of renewal decision
- Process contract paperwork
- Align on success metrics for next year

## Renewal Risk Indicators
- Health score below 50 at 90-day mark
- Champion change in last 90 days
- More than 3 unresolved support tickets
- Active users below 60% of licensed seats

## Renewal Discount Policy
- Health score 50-70: up to 5% loyalty discount available
- Health score 30-50: up to 15% retention discount (requires VP approval)
- Health score below 30: escalate to executive team for custom terms

## Champion Change Protocol
When a champion resigns or departs:
1. Identify new stakeholder within 5 business days
2. Schedule executive introduction meeting
3. Provide personalized platform overview
4. Offer dedicated onboarding for the new champion
