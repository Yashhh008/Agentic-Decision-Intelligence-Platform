# NimbusCRM — Renewal FAQ

## General Renewal Questions

**Q: When does my contract renewal process begin?**
A: NimbusCRM sends an initial renewal notification 90 days before the contract end date. Your CSM will reach out to schedule a renewal review meeting at this time.

**Q: What happens if we don't renew by the contract end date?**
A: Contracts that lapse enter a 15-day grace period during which the account remains active. After 15 days, the account is suspended. After 30 days, data may be purged. We strongly recommend renewing in advance to avoid disruption.

**Q: Can we renew month-to-month?**
A: Month-to-month renewals are only available on the Starter tier. Professional and Enterprise tiers require annual commitments. Contact your CSM to discuss options.

**Q: Can we reduce our seat count at renewal?**
A: Yes, seat reductions are permitted at renewal with 30 days written notice before the contract end date. Mid-contract seat reductions are not available.

---

## Pricing Questions

**Q: Will our price increase at renewal?**
A: NimbusCRM applies an annual price adjustment of up to 5% for standard renewals. Customers who renew 90+ days early qualify for a price lock (no increase).

**Q: Can we negotiate pricing at renewal?**
A: Your CSM can discuss pricing during the renewal process. Any discounts beyond 5% require CSM Manager approval. See the Pricing & Discount Policy for full details.

**Q: Are multi-year discounts available?**
A: Yes. Customers committing to a 2-year renewal qualify for a 10% discount. 3-year commitments qualify for 15%. Contact your CSM for details.

---

## Process Questions

**Q: Who signs the renewal contract?**
A: The renewal order form is sent via DocuSign to the authorized signatory on file. Please notify your CSM if the signatory has changed.

**Q: Can we add seats or upgrade tier at renewal?**
A: Yes, both seat additions and tier upgrades can be combined with renewal to simplify billing. Your CSM will prepare a single renewal order form covering all changes.

**Q: What if our champion or key contact has left the company?**
A: Please notify your CSM immediately if a key contact has departed. We will follow our Champion Transition Playbook to ensure continuity. Prior verbal commitments from departed employees are not binding and all renewal terms will be confirmed with the new contact.

**Q: How long does the renewal process take?**
A: Standard renewals take 5–7 business days from quote to signature. Complex renewals (tier upgrades, large seat additions, custom terms) may take 2–3 weeks. Start early to avoid service gaps.

---

## Technical Questions

**Q: Will there be any downtime during renewal?**
A: No. Renewal is a billing and contract event only — there is no service interruption.

**Q: What happens to our data if we don't renew?**
A: Data is retained for 30 days post-contract. After 30 days, data is permanently deleted per our data retention policy. Data export is available up until the account is suspended.
