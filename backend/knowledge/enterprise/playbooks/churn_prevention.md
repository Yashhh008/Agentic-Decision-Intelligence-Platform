# NimbusCRM Churn Prevention Playbook

## Purpose
This playbook guides Customer Success Managers in preventing churn for at-risk accounts.

## When to Activate
- Health Score drops below 40
- Customer expresses dissatisfaction in meeting or email
- Renewal is within 60 days and health is below 60
- Multiple support tickets with no resolution
- Champion has left the organization

## Required Actions (in order)

### Step 1: Executive Escalation
- Notify VP of Customer Success within 24 hours
- Schedule executive-to-executive call within 5 business days
- Do NOT attempt to handle critical churn risk at CSM level alone

### Step 2: Root Cause Analysis
- Review all open support tickets
- Review last 3 meeting transcripts
- Identify top 3 pain points from customer interactions
- Confirm adoption metrics (active vs licensed users)

### Step 3: Recovery Plan
- Create a 30-day success plan with the customer
- Assign dedicated support engineer for 60 days
- Schedule weekly check-ins
- Offer complimentary onboarding workshop

### Step 4: Renewal Commitment
- Do not discuss pricing increases during recovery period
- Offer renewal discount if health score is below 30
- Escalate to Sales team if customer requests contract changes

## Success Metrics
- Health score improvement from below 40 to above 60 within 60 days
- Active user ratio exceeds 70% of licensed users
- No new escalated support tickets within 30 days
