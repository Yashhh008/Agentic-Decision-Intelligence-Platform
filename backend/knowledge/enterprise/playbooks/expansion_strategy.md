# NimbusCRM Expansion & Upsell Playbook

## Purpose
Identify and execute upsell and expansion opportunities.

## Expansion Signals to Watch
- Active user ratio exceeds 90% of licensed seats
- Customer mentions growing teams in meetings
- Customer requests features only available in higher tier
- Customer integrates with 3+ third-party tools
- Quarterly revenue growth exceeding 20%

## Expansion Products

### NimbusCRM Professional → Enterprise Upgrade
Trigger: Customer has 50+ users or needs advanced reporting
Value proposition: Unlimited custom reports, AI Insights dashboard, priority support
Typical uplift: 40-60% ARR increase

### NimbusCRM Add-ons
- AI Insights Module: Predictive analytics and automated scoring
- Advanced Integrations Pack: Salesforce, HubSpot, Jira, Zendesk
- Custom Onboarding Pack: Dedicated specialist for 6 months

## Upsell Process
1. Identify expansion signal from CRM or meeting transcript
2. Validate with usage data (confirm seats nearly full)
3. Prepare ROI analysis showing value of upgrade
4. Present to champion first, then economic buyer
5. Coordinate with Sales team for contract modification

## Timing Recommendations
- Best time to present upsell: During positive QBR
- Avoid: During active support escalation
- Avoid: Within 30 days of renewal (unless customer-initiated)
