# NimbusCRM — Service Level Agreement (SLA) Policy

## Overview
This document outlines the service level commitments NimbusCRM provides to customers by tier, and the internal CSM response guidelines.

## Platform Uptime SLAs

| Tier | Uptime Guarantee | Credit for Breach |
|---|---|---|
| Starter | 99.5% | 10% monthly credit |
| Professional | 99.7% | 15% monthly credit |
| Enterprise | 99.9% | 25% monthly credit |
| Enterprise Plus | 99.95% | 30% monthly credit + exec call |

## Support Response SLAs

### Starter
- Email support only
- Response time: 2 business days
- No weekend support

### Professional
- Email + chat support
- Response time: 8 business hours
- Weekend support: Email only

### Enterprise
- Email, chat, and phone support
- Response time: 4 business hours for standard, 1 hour for critical
- 24/7 support for P0/P1 incidents
- Dedicated CSM included

### Enterprise Plus
- All Enterprise benefits
- Named senior support engineer
- 30-minute response for P0 incidents
- Quarterly SLA review meetings

## Incident Priority Levels

| Priority | Definition | Response Time |
|---|---|---|
| P0 - Critical | Platform completely unavailable | 30 minutes |
| P1 - High | Major feature broken, data loss risk | 1 hour |
| P2 - Medium | Feature degraded, workaround exists | 4 hours |
| P3 - Low | Minor issue, cosmetic, or feature request | 2 business days |

## CSM Responsibilities During Incidents
- For P0/P1 incidents affecting your accounts: proactively notify the customer before they report it
- Send incident update every 30 minutes during active P0 incidents
- Prepare post-incident summary within 48 hours for Enterprise/Enterprise Plus accounts
- Log all SLA-impacting incidents in CRM under the account

## SLA Credit Claim Process
1. Customer must file credit request within 30 days of the incident
2. CSM validates against internal incident log
3. Credits applied to next invoice — not issued as cash refunds
4. VP Customer Success must approve credits > $5,000
